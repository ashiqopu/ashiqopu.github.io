About Me

I am a graduate assistant at The University of Arizona and pursuing my doctorate degree in Computer Science. I am currently working in the Network Research Lab (NRL) with Dr. Beichuan Zhang and focusing on application and challenges of Named Data Networking in ad-hoc networks and disruption tolerant networks. I love playing badminton, watching movies, sightseeing, taking pictures, and following latest tech news.
